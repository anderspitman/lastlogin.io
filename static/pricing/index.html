Pricing

LastLogin is intended to be free for users, and free for most organizations.

However, for organizations with high numbers of users, we may reach out to negotiate a fair payment plan. If you think you might fall into this category, please reach out ahead of time. If a specific redirect target is generating a large amount of traffic and we are unable to contact the owners, we maybe throttle that redirect.

Donate

LastLogin is primarily donation-funded. If you find it useful, or simply want it to succeed, please consider donating to the Patreon.
